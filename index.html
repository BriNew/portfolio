I am a Web Developer based in Madison, WI. I am interested in language, spoken or otherwise. I love to travel, the more unfamiliar the place the better. I enjoy teaching and learning, bridging the gap between knowledge and interest, cultures, and language.

My love for language is rooted in a fascination in communication. Living in other countries and having co-workers from dozens of other countries puts a focus on non-verbal communication, and eventually the importance of learning other verbal languages. I started coding while teaching and developing curriculum for the medical english program at the University of Health Sciences in Vientiane, Laos. Lao is not a widespread language, and has a minimal presence on the web. Resources our team needed didn't exist, I learned to code and continue to support their efforts.
